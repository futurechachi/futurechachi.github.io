Bio

Jody Frandle

Dawn Corine

Jody Frandle and Dawn Corine have been comedy partners and the best of friends since the turn of the century. They instantly bonded over 80's music, adventures and creating stories on stage that inspire gutteral laughter. Their shows are a mix of short and long form improv with a focus on diving deep into character and trusting the journey with the audience. They've performed for years together in Chicken Scratch, Drunken Monkey and Johnny Kats and are excited to share their duo comedy fun.

History

  • 2000-2002

    Being the Chicken

    Jody is pushed by friends into trying out for Dawn's improv group, The Johnny Cats. It is her first time doing improv. She is asked to be a chicken and joins the group.

  • 2002-2006

    Drunken Monkey

    Dawn and Jody break off from Johnny Cats and form Drunken Monkey, a short-form improv group in San Francisco. Beyond improv, their shows feature short sketch comedy and parody songs.

  • 2006-Present

    Return of the Chicken

    The dynamic duo merge with members of Blue Blanket Improv to form the supergroup, Chicken Scratch. Among numerous self-produced runs of shows, they perform ## back-to-back years at the SF Improv Festival.

  • Into the Future

    Our heroines begin thinking about doing a two women show, but they are unsure if audiences can handle this greatness.

  • Nov 2014

    Going Bi-Coastal

    Amid concerns of the power of these two performing together for a full show, the Department of Homeland Security steps in a requests that Dawn move to the East coast. The agency's hope is that by separating them, the duo's power can be contained.

  • Jun 2016

    See the Power

    Despite safety concerns, Jody and Dawn rejoin in SF for performance at the Exit Theater. Will the world survive? Be there and find out.
